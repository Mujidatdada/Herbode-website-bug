Our Programs

Every Program.
Every Woman. Every Chance.

From education sponsorships to festive community drives, each initiative creates lasting, dignified change for women and girls across Nigeria.

📚
Educate A Girl Child

We directly sponsor girls who face financial or social barriers to schooling — covering tuition and educational materials so they never have to choose between survival and learning.

✦ Currently sponsoring 5 girls

Education is the most powerful tool we can place in a girl’s hands. Help us place it.

💛
₦550 Campaign

₦550 is all it takes to send a girl to school for one full day. This community-powered micro-giving initiative makes everyone a part of the solution.

✦ One school day = ₦550

Small gifts. Massive ripple. Join the growing community of everyday heroes.

🎄
Herbode Christmas Drive

No woman or child should feel forgotten during the holidays. Our annual drive distributes food packages and clothing to hundreds of women and children in underserved communities.

✦ 300+ women & children reached

Every item donated says: you are seen, you are valued, you matter.

🗣
Monthly X Sensitization Sessions

Each month we host community sensitization sessions on women’s rights, mental health, safety, and economic empowerment — in spaces where women can speak freely without judgment.

✦ Monthly community sessions

Awareness is the first step to change. These conversations save lives.

Our Story

Built on Compassion.
Driven by Purpose.

Herbode Foundation was born from a simple, urgent belief: no woman should face her hardest moments alone. We exist to be the safe haven every woman deserves — structured, compassionate, and community-rooted.

🛡
Safety
Every woman has the right to feel protected and secure.
🌿
Empowerment
We build capacity, not dependency.
🤝
Community
Change is sustained by people, not programmes.
⭐
Dignity
Support that honors her humanity, always.
Our Mission

“To be a safe space for women and girls in need, empowering them through shelter, education, and community-driven support to rebuild their lives with dignity and strength.”

Our Vision

“A society where every woman is safe, empowered, and supported with access to shelter and opportunities for growth.”

Our Structure
